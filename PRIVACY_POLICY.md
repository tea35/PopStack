# Privacy Policy for PopStack

**Last updated: May 15, 2026**

Thank you for choosing to use PopStack. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. The short answer is: **PopStack does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.**

## 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect any personal information.**
PopStack operates entirely within your browser.
- The URLs and titles of the pages you save are securely stored using Google Chrome's native Bookmarks API (`chrome.bookmarks`).
- Your preferences (such as notification settings and scheduled times) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 2. How Your Information Is Shared
Because we do not collect any data, we cannot share it.
However, please note that if you have "Google Chrome Sync" enabled in your browser, your bookmarks and extension settings may be synced across your devices by Google, subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We have no access to this synced data.

## 3. Third-Party Access
PopStack does not use any third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), tracking tools, or external databases. Your browsing activity remains entirely private and stays on your machine.

## 4. Required Permissions
To function properly, PopStack requires the following minimal permissions in your browser:
- **`bookmarks`**: To save and remove articles you want to read later, and to fetch an article for your daily notification.
- **`storage`**: To save your extension settings (e.g., notification time, toggle state).
- **`alarms` & `notifications`**: To schedule and display the daily reminder to read your saved articles.
- **`contextMenus`**: To allow you to comfortably save links via the right-click menu.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time to stay compliant with relevant laws and guidelines. We will notify you of any major changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
